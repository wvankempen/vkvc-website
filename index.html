I · Principles An approach

We work selectively, and at depth.

Van Kempen Ventures advises a small number of clients at any one time. Our remit ranges from a single board conversation to a multi-year engagement; what is constant is the standard. Independent perspective. Careful judgement. The discipline to say less when less is what is needed.
