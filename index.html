I · Principles An approach

Hands-on, not arm’s-length.

We work with our hands on the keyboard, not just on the whiteboard. Whether building, advising, or backing, the discipline is the same: clear outcomes over thick decks, simple over clever, and AI as a working colleague rather than a marketing word.

“We’re not a firm in the institutional sense. We’re operators who build with our clients, advise alongside their teams, and invest in the ones we believe in.”

II · The work Three modes

Three ways we work.

i.

Build

Software products as ventures. AI‑native by design, built fast, shipped early, measured by retention.

ii.

Advise

Working hands-on with companies building AI‑native products. We come in at moments of scale or pivot, when a product has traction but needs sharper focus to grow, retain customers, or improve unit economics. Product vision and journey design alongside the team, not consulting from the outside. AI as a working colleague.

iii.

Invest

Backing founders building the products we’d otherwise build ourselves. Cheques sized for early stages, alongside the work, not in place of it.
